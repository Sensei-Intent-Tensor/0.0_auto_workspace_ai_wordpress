# Blogs (Derived from Deep Research)

**Hierarchy:** Deep Research (PARENT) → Blogs (CHILD) → Social (GRANDCHILD)

---

## Purpose

Blogs are **shallow explanations** derived from deep research. They translate mathematical derivations into accessible narratives for:
- Technical founders
- Product managers  
- Business operators
- Investors

**Critical Rule:** Blogs CANNOT contradict deep research. They simplify and explain, but truth originates upstream.

---

## Derivation Process

```
Deep Research Article
  ↓ (extract core insight)
Blog Post  
  ↓ (distill to actionable)
Social Media Post
```

### Example Flow

**Deep Research:**
```
Article: "Five-Department Business Mod Architecture"
Core math: ∑σᵢ < ℒ_total (firm stability constraint)
Derivation: 47 pages of tensor mechanics
```

**↓ Derives to Blog:**
```
Title: "Why Your Startup Has Exactly 5 Key Roles"
Insight: Fewer than 5 = critical σ unmanaged, More than 5 = coordination overhead
Length: 1,500 words, no equations
Audience: Founders building teams
```

**↓ Derives to Social:**
```
Tweet: "Your company needs exactly 5 departments. Not 4. Not 6. Here's why..."
Thread: 8 tweets explaining σ-suppression in plain language
```

## Writing Standards

### Tone
- Confident, not arrogant
- Accessible, not dumbed down
- Practical, not purely theoretical

### Structure
```markdown
# Title (Insight-driven, not jargon)

## The Problem (relatable scenario)
## Why This Happens (simplified math)
## What To Do (actionable steps)
## Real Example (BusinessGPT or case study)
```

### Length
- Short: 800-1,200 words (quick insights)
- Medium: 1,500-2,500 words (deep dives)
- Long: 3,000-5,000 words (comprehensive guides)

### Mathematics
- Minimal equations (1-2 max)
- Use analogies and diagrams
- Link to deep research for full derivations

## Publishing Workflow

1. **Identify deep research article** to derive from
2. **Extract core insight** (the "aha" moment)
3. **Write blog draft** (no math jargon)
4. **Add real example** (BusinessGPT or case study)
5. **Link to parent research** (for those who want depth)
6. **Review against standards** (tone, length, actionability)
7. **Publish to WordPress** (auto-workspace-ai.com/f/articles/)

---
